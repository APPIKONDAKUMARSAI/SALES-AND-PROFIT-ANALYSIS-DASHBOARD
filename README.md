# 🛒 Retail Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Retail Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project developed to analyze and visualize retail business performance across multiple dimensions such as **Sales, Profit, Quantity, Customer Segments, Shipping Modes, Regions, and Product Categories**.

This dashboard transforms raw retail transaction data into meaningful business insights, enabling stakeholders to:

* Monitor key performance indicators (KPIs)
* Identify profitable and loss-making areas
* Analyze customer purchasing behavior
* Evaluate shipping and operational performance
* Support strategic business decision-making through data visualization

# 📌 Key Business Insights

* 🌎 **West Region** generated the highest sales and overall profit.
* 🚚 **Standard Class** shipping handled the majority of customer orders.
* 💻 **Technology Category** contributed significantly to total revenue.
* 📉 Several products generated high sales but low profit due to heavy discounts.
* 👥 **Consumer Segment** had the highest contribution to total orders and sales volume.
